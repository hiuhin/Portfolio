Where Is The Love?

Built using JavaScript, HTML5, Canvas, and CSS3, Where Is The Love is a quick-reaction game based on Super Mario Party minigame "Looking for Love". Upon starting the game, the four shapes representing the suits of playing card appears randomly on the top, bottom, left, and right spots.
